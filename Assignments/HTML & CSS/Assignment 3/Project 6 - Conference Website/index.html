Venue

  Hilton San Francisco Union Square

Centrally-located in San Francisco’s Union Square, steps away from our subway and muni lines, the Hilton is one of the largest and most versatile event spaces in the city. We’ve reserved an entire ballroom floor and lounge to comfortably seat 600 guests in the general sessions, with a separate dedicated space to seat 600 in dining rounds. Talks are presented with bright and crisp 6k projectors, and we have a dedicated lounge space for activities & mingling with attendees and sponsors, along with separate rooms for breakout discussions and unconference sessions.
